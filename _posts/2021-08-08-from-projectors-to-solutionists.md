Anton Howes has been doing *good history* on his substack. Painstaking attention to detail to try and get to the likeliest view of a matter. Free from arroagance. It's a delight to read. 

This recent [post](https://antonhowes.substack.com/p/golden-age-of-invention-rise-of-the) on the framing and naming of those people who make significant improvements at the margin does the useful job of showing how words and concepts change over time. It reminded me of the way Ian Hacking deployed the notion of the envinronmental niche in his book Mad Travellers.

EBUTT files are now the standard deliverable for iPlayer captions. This has resulted in standardised positioning and other improved presentational aspects. It removes STL files from this workflow entirely. Delighted to be able to support the BBC on this. 

First programme [here](https://www.bbc.co.uk/iplayer/episode/p08gdwcc/bitesize-1314-year-olds-week-7-1-writing-techniques); it's a BBC Bitesize programme on... writing techniques! Highly appropriate.

Listening to amaarae again, the style has that flavour of the future i want
* light, carried on breezes almost
* but has that *intentionality* amaarae talks about in an interview https://www.vulture.com/article/amaarae-fountain-baby-influences-interview.html
* the use of fragments that avalon emerson talked about. fragments of fashion, fragments of social media speak, fragments of love, images

and listening to *Wasted Eyes*, hearing her sing “i can’t be your love, too many things to lose” gave me fleetingly but vividly a sense of a through line of a person’s story of failed love.

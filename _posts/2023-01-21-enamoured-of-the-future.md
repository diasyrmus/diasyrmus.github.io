> A young man so enamoured of the future that they seem to mirror the future of anyone who encounters them  
> **Les Onze** - Pierre Michon

That phrase, 'enamoured of the future' has been ringing round my head. It seems so alien. What I actually feel, what I seem to see in the world around me, is anxiety, a fear of the future. That is a personal feeling, but it is also the feeling of a western society faced with climate change, and its demise. A one-way street.
What would it mean, what does it mean to be enamoured of the future? We do not need to believe that the future is rosy to be enamoured of the next week, of the next year, to look at the world around us and for it to generate a spark in the eye, in the mind and to know, to a degree, that the future is ours.
And so I have been saying the phrase to myself, almost like a mantra, at the beginning of 2023. To try and think, how can I order my life to create this feeling?

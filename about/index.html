tom wootton

head of product and portfolio for accessibility services at Red Bee Media - opinions and thoughts are entirely my own here of course. My background wasn't broadcasting engineering, or media, but language and literature, and a good deal of loafing around. But I've been a captioner, trainer, test manager and product manager, and one area where I think I'm strong is connecting the nuts and bolts of technology to the bigger picture of what we do and why we do it, and also bringing in concepts and thoughts from outside the immediate industry thinking.

Because I've never really grown up in the media industry, it's always been important to test my thinking out, work it through, look at it from different angles - why do we do things this way? is this the best way of doing things? could we be doing things differently? So this is a scratchpad for stray thoughts, blurts and jottings on media, product and accessibility.
